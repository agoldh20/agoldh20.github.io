About Me

I'm Adam Goldwater and I'm a chef turned coder. When cooking in the kitchen, its common to yell "HOT!" and its not uncommon for me to still do that when I've finished a dev task.

As you can see, the kitchen mentality has shifted into my coding. Instead of playing with things that go on a plate I play with what I put on your browser. Personally I think my results are just as yummy.

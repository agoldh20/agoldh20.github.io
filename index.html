About

I'm Adam Goldwater and I'm a chef turned coder. One of my favorite expressions in the kitchen is, "Let's see if I can do this, now I'm curious!"

This is the mentality that shifted me into coding and instead of playing with things that go on a plate, I play with what I put on your browser. Personally I think my results are just as yummy.
